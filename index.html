R&B MUSIC

R&B also known as Rythm And Blues the right type of music that touches the soul. It is a perform of music that originated in African American communities in the 1940s. The term was originally used by record companies to describe recordings marketed predominantly to urban African Americans, R&B has influenced many genres of music such as pop, hip hop, and rock. It has also become an escape for people, no matter what they are going through. Just being able to listen to the music and relate to the lyrics feels like a great escape.
